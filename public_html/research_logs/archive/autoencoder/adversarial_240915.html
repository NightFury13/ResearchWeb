Sanity Check

To see what's really going wrong, I added a guassian noise to the input images (mean=0, std-dev=5) and the average reconstruction loss obtained for these images on the autoencoder is about 102.44 (L2-norm). Which is infact much lesser than the average reconstruction loss of original images (the ones which the autoencoder was trained upon).
